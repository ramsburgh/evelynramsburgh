From Problem to Product

Step 1

Understand

Clear alignment between business goals, user needs, and technical constraints sets the foundation for every design decision.

Step 2

Explore / Validate

Multiple solutions are explored through flows, wireframes, and prototypes, allowing early validation and reducing risk.

Step 3

Design / Build

Validated ideas are translated into thoughtful, accessible, and scalable UI, ensuring consistency across screens and systems.

Step 4

Deliver / Improve

Design and engineering work closely through implementation, QA, and iteration, refining the product based on real usage.
