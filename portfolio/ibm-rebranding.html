IBM Seller Incentives — App Icon Redesign

Design of the official application icon for IBM Seller Incentives, a global internal platform used by ~70,000+ IBM Sellers and Sales Managers worldwide.

Category Enterprise · Branding
Role Senior Product Designer
Status Shipped · IBM Approved

Context

During my time at IBM, I led the redesign of the official app icon for IBM Seller Incentives. The application had recently transitioned to a cloud-based environment, creating the need for an updated visual identity that aligned with IBM’s modern app ecosystem while preserving recognition for existing users.

Impact

The final icon was approved by IBM’s Branding team and adopted as the official app icon, supporting consistent recognition across IBM’s internal platforms and reinforcing trust among a large, globally distributed sales organization.

The Challenge

The challenge was to design a new app icon that balanced strict enterprise branding constraints with functional clarity. The icon needed to comply fully with IBM App Icon Guidelines, remain legible at small sizes, and visually communicate concepts related to sales performance, incentives, and calculations—without breaking IBM’s established visual system.

Discovery & Insights

The discovery phase focused on understanding both brand governance and product intent. I reviewed IBM’s app icon system, audited existing internal icons, and analyzed the former Seller Incentives icon to identify elements worth preserving.

  • Brand consistency and scalability were non-negotiable.
  • The icon needed to feel native to IBM’s cloud ecosystem.
  • Visual continuity was key to avoid disrupting existing user recognition.

Approach & Solution

I explored multiple icon concepts using IBM’s grid system, stroke rules, and color constraints. Visual metaphors related to sales, incentives, and cloud infrastructure were abstracted into a simple, scalable form that could function across platforms.

  • Aligned geometry, stroke weight, and spacing with IBM standards.
  • Tested legibility at small sizes and across themes.
  • Iterated based on stakeholder and branding feedback.

Final Result

The final IBM Seller Incentives app icon clearly represents the product’s purpose while fully adhering to IBM’s brand governance. It supports recognition across internal tools and contributes to a cohesive enterprise application ecosystem.

Availability in IBM Design Library

The IBM Seller Incentives app icon is currently part of IBM’s official App Icon Library and can be consulted here .
