Skills

I define product direction by understanding user needs, business goals, and technical constraints. This includes discovery, research synthesis, user flows, and problem framing before jumping into solutions.

I design clear, accessible, and scalable interfaces, translating complex requirements into intuitive UI. My focus is on usability, visual clarity, and systems that scale with the product.

I collaborate closely with engineering to define design systems, components, and handoff processes, ensuring consistency, accessibility, and efficient implementation across teams.

I contribute to product direction by aligning design decisions with business goals, technical feasibility, and delivery timelines. This includes prioritization, stakeholder alignment, and cross-functional leadership.

I help define and evolve brand identities through visual language, typography, color, and layout, ensuring the brand feels cohesive, intentional, and aligned with the product experience.

I create clean, visually consistent assets that support both product and brand, including layouts, marketing materials, and supporting graphics designed for clarity and reuse.

I edit short-form and product-focused videos, prioritizing clarity, pacing, and visual coherence, often supporting demos, presentations, and educational or marketing content.

I work with basic front-end development to bring designs to life, including landing pages and product pages. This helps ensure feasibility, clean implementation, and smooth collaboration with engineering.
